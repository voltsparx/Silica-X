# Code of Conduct

## 1. Purpose

Sylica-X is committed to maintaining a respectful, safe, and professional community for contributors and users.

## 2. Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:

- Being respectful in language and tone.
- Providing constructive feedback.
- Accepting accountability and learning from mistakes.
- Focusing on technical issues rather than personal attacks.
- Respecting legal and ethical boundaries for OSINT usage.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, insults, trolling, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Encouraging illegal activity, abuse, or unauthorized targeting.
- Any behavior that violates applicable laws or platform terms.

## 3. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- Repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and reviews.
- Project chat channels or community spaces linked to this repository.
- Public interactions where an individual is representing this project.

## 4. Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may take any appropriate action in response to behavior that violates this policy, including:

- Warning the contributor.
- Removing comments, issues, or pull requests.
- Temporarily or permanently restricting participation.

## 5. Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, contact:

All reports will be reviewed in good faith and handled as promptly and confidentially as practical.

## 6. Attribution

This policy is inspired by common open-source community conduct standards, including Contributor Covenant principles, adapted for the Sylica-X project context.
